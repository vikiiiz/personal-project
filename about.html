About Me!

Hello! I'm Vikram, a passionate Balinese guitarist who discovered my love for music in grade 8. My musical journey began with the basics - learning chords and proper guitar techniques. Through dedicated practice and guidance from my music teacher, I've grown from a beginner to a performer.

One of my proudest moments was performing "So Far Away" by Avenged Sevenfold at the MaxOne Hotel concert. This experience inspired me to create my own covers, starting with simpler pieces like "Blue" by Yung Kai and "21 Guns" by Green Day. As I progressed, I delved into learning scales to enhance my playing technique and musical expression.

My journey as a guitarist continues to evolve, filled with challenges and achievements. Through this website, I share my musical journey, covers, and the joy of playing guitar. Each performance and recording is a step forward in my musical development, and I'm excited to share this journey with fellow music enthusiasts.
